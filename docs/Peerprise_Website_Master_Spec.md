# Peerprise Website Master Specification

## Status

This document is the approved content and design direction for the Peerprise website. It is intended to be implemented page by page in Cursor.

The website will position **managed digital presence as the primary offer**, while presenting **custom software development and engineering partnerships as high-value secondary services**.

SEO campaigns, traffic generation, paid advertising, marketing strategy, lead generation and analytics services are outside the Peerprise offer and should not be claimed anywhere on the website.

## Approved visual amendment — preserve the existing hero

The existing homepage hero colour scheme, gradients, background treatment and overall visual identity are approved and must be preserved. The remainder of the website should be designed around that hero rather than replacing it with a generic light-theme identity.

The site should use a controlled hybrid theme: the current hero as the primary brand anchor, light neutral sections for long-form readability, and selective dark sections derived from the hero palette. Existing hero structure or artwork should only be changed for a demonstrated responsive, accessibility or content-integrity reason.

Where later colour examples in this document conflict with the existing hero, extract and reuse the actual colours from the current implementation. The colour values listed later are fallback guidance only, not instructions to replace the approved hero palette.

When a client needs to improve a workflow, connect systems or build something custom, the same team can provide experienced software engineering and delivery support.

**Run what you have. Improve what is slowing you down. Build what you need next.**

## One-sentence company description

Peerprise provides managed website and social support, digital operations, custom software development and outsourced engineering capacity through one accountable team.

## Audience

Peerprise serves three primary groups:

1. **Small businesses and professionals** that need a reliable team to maintain their website, social presence and day-to-day digital operations.
2. **Growing companies** that need integrations, automation, platform improvements or custom software.
3. **Agencies and product teams** that need dependable development capacity, specialist engineering skills or white-label delivery.

## Brand tone

The writing should be:

- Clear and calm
- Confident without exaggeration
- Practical rather than promotional
- Technically credible
- Direct about scope and limitations
- Written in international British English

Avoid:

- “Cutting-edge solutions”
- “We bring your vision to life”
- “Transform your business”
- “Skyrocket your growth”
- “Guaranteed results”
- “Best-in-class”
- Unsupported claims about enterprise scale, security, traffic or revenue
- Language that makes Peerprise sound like a low-cost social media posting service

# 2. Service Architecture

Peerprise will present five connected service lines.

## 2.1 Website Care and Support

Ongoing maintenance, updates, troubleshooting and technical ownership for existing websites.

## 2.2 Social Presence Support

Planned content, branded post graphics, scheduling, publishing and profile upkeep. This is an operational presence service, not a growth-marketing or advertising service.

## 2.3 Digital Operations

Support for the forms, accounts, integrations, workflows and third-party tools behind a business’s digital presence.

## 2.4 Custom Software

Web applications, internal tools, portals, APIs, integrations, automation and platform modernisation.

## 2.5 Engineering Partnerships

Dedicated, project-based or white-label engineering delivery for agencies, software companies and larger organisations.

## Service progression

- **Run:** website care, publishing and support
- **Improve:** integrations, automation and operational improvements
- **Build:** custom applications and platforms
- **Scale:** dedicated or white-label engineering capacity

## FAQ

### Can you take over an existing website?

Yes. Peerprise first reviews the platform, hosting, access, update status and any existing technical risks. A stabilisation scope may be recommended before regular support begins.

### Do unused support hours roll over?

No unless the written proposal states otherwise. Monthly capacity is reserved for the selected period and is not automatically carried forward.

### Can you redesign or rebuild our website?

Yes, but redesigns and full builds are separate projects rather than routine monthly support tasks.

### Do you provide emergency support?

Emergency and out-of-hours support is available only through a separate agreement with defined coverage and response expectations.

Use the existing hero palette as the source of truth. Audit the current implementation and extract reusable tokens for its background, gradients, accent colours, foreground text, borders and interactive states.

Required token roles:

- Elevated light surface
- Primary ink and secondary text
- Muted text and neutral border
- Hero dark surface
- Hero foreground text
- Primary accent derived from the hero
- Accent hover and soft accent states
- Focus, success, warning and error states

Do not introduce unrelated accent colours or replace the current hero gradients. The site should feel visually connected to the existing hero across every page.

# 24. Final Approved Positioning Summary

Peerprise should not present itself as a marketing agency that also happens to employ developers.

Peerprise should present itself as:

> **An engineering-led digital partner that manages a business’s day-to-day digital presence and provides serious technical delivery when the requirement grows.**

The primary acquisition offer remains:

> **Your entire digital presence, managed by one reliable team.**

The secondary commercial routes are:

- Custom software for growing businesses
- Engineering partnerships for agencies and product teams

This structure allows Peerprise to win accessible recurring support engagements without weakening its credibility as a professional software engineering company.
